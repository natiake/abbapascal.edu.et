Our Mission & History

Rooted in faith, striving for academic excellence since 1985.

Our Mission

Abba Pascal Catholic School is committed to providing a superior education in a safe, Christ-centered environment. We prepare students for lifelong learning, leadership, and service to God and community.

Our Vision

To be the premier educational institution in the region, recognized for academic excellence, deep spiritual formation, and the development of compassionate, ethical leaders.

Our Legacy

Founded in **1985** by the Sisters of the Holy Cross Parish, our school carries a legacy of dedicated service and education. We started with just two classrooms and have grown into a comprehensive **K-12 institution**, committed to serving the families of Wolaita Sodo and beyond. Our history is a testament to the enduring power of faith and community investment in education.

Our Catholic Identity

Faith is the cornerstone of all we do. From weekly Mass and religious education integrated into every grade level to our focus on virtues and social justice, we guide students on their spiritual journey, helping them understand their role in the Church and the world.

Academics & Curriculum

A comprehensive K-12 program designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare students for university success.

Primary School (K-6)

Focus on foundational literacy, numeracy, and a blend of Ethiopian and international curricula. Introduction to Computer Science basics and weekly Theology classes.

Middle School (7-8)

Transition to specialized subjects, critical thinking, and enhanced focus on STEM. Students begin service learning projects.

High School (9-12)

University preparatory track with Advanced Placement options, career guidance, and intensive study in core subject areas.

Key Departments

  • Mathematics & Statistics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Theology & Ethics
  • English & Amharic Literature
  • History & Geography
  • Arts & Music
  • Information Technology

Admissions: How to Apply

We welcome students who are ready to embrace our values of faith and academic pursuit.

4 Simple Steps to Enrollment

1

Initial Inquiry & Visit

Fill out the inquiry form on this page or call us to schedule a personal tour of our campus.

2

Application Submission

Submit the completed application form along with all required documents (see sidebar) before the deadline.

3

Assessment & Interview

The student will participate in a placement assessment and a family interview with the Principal or Vice-Principal.

4

Acceptance & Enrollment

Upon acceptance, pay the enrollment deposit to secure your child's place in the class.

Required Documents

  • Previous two years of academic transcripts/report cards.
  • Copy of birth certificate or national ID.
  • Two letters of recommendation (one academic, one personal/pastoral).
  • Passport-size photograph of the student.
